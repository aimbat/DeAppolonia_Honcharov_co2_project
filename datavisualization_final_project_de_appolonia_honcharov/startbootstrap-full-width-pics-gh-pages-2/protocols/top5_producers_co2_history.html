Historical CO2 Production of top 5 world CO2 producers

Visualization protocol

TIMESTAMP

1860-2021

DATA SOURCE

https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/toriqulstu/global-socio-economic-and-environmental-indicators

This comprehensive dataset provides a rich collection of socio-economic and environmental indicators for countries across the world. Spanning the years from 1990 to 2021, the dataset includes valuable information on Human Development Index (HDI), Life Expectancy, Gross National Income per Capita (GNI), and CO2 Production.

Provenance: hdr.undp.org

License: CC0: Public Domain

https://github.com/owid/co2-data

Complete CO2 and Greenhouse Gas Emissions dataset is a collection of key metrics maintained by Our World in Data. It is updated regularly and includes data on CO2 emissions (annual, per capita, cumulative and consumption-based).

 

DATASET

Top 5 world CO2 producers

https://drive.google.com/file/d/1Cbr3YzTC5F4QnVffW_L4ho5EdfigHuHR/view?usp=sharing

METADATA

year: the date from 1860-2021

country: representing the name of top 5 world co2 producers with CO2 values per year.

PROTOCOL

1.     Data Concatenation from Multiple Sources:

 

Four distinct datasets were sourced from Kaggle and subsequently concatenated into a single comprehensive dataset.

This consolidated dataset was then further combined with an additional dataset obtained from GitHub.

 

2.     Data Preprocessing and Feature Selection:

 

Identified the features relevant to the research question, including 'year', 'co2',

'Country'.

Considered domain knowledge and research objectives when selecting features.

Additionally, a thorough check for missing values was performed, and it was observed that the dataset did not contain any missing values.

 

3.     Data Visualization Using Flourish:

 

Represented the flow of CO2 emissions of the top 5 CO2 producers from 1860 to 2021 using a flowchart.

Analyzed CO2 emissions data for each country.

Identified the top 5 countries with the most significant changes in CO2 emissions over the specified period.

Utilized a tool like Flourish to create an informative and visually appealing flowchart illustrating the historical CO2 production of top 5 world CO2 producers.
